WELCOME TO PARADISE RESORT

















Set amidst a tropical landscape, Paradise Resort is located in Kumbakonam. It features 2 dining options, an outdoor pool and air-conditioned rooms with a private balcony. Free Wi-Fi and parking are provided. Overlooking the pool or Arasalar River, rooms at Paradise come with antique doors and traditional teak wooden furnishings. All are equipped with a cable TV and private bathroom with shower facilities. Select bathrooms have a bathtub. Guests can enjoy a relaxing body massage at the spa, or take a stroll in the neighbouring village. With a 24-hour front desk, the resort also provides airport shuttle services. Yazhi Restaurant serves a fine selection of local and European dishes. Other dining choices include a tree-top dining experience, with vegetarian and non-vegetarian meal options. Resort Paradise is 1 km from Darasuram Iravadeswarar Temple and 4 km from Kumbakonam Railway Station. Tanjore Railway Station is 35 km away. We speak your language!!!
